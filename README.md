# evenstone

evenstone is a series of photographs of Diamond Head, inspired by Monet's Rouen Cathedral series.

The Rouen Cathedral series is my favorite of Monet's.

He created over 30 paintings of the cathedral in different lighting conditions and scenes.

A reflection of his inspired this work:

> Everything changes, even stone.

In Hawaii, I stood in the same place on Waikiki Beach throughout the course of a day. The photographs displayed here are photos I took on that day, as the light and scene changed.
